Caves In Patagonia, Chile

The Marble Caves (also called the Capillas de Mármol) is one of the best-hidden gems in Chile’s Patagonia region. If you are planning a trip down the scenic Carretera Austral Highway in southern Chile, the Marble Caves is a must! These beautiful caves are located in the middle of General Carrera Lake and can be only accessed by boat or kayak.
